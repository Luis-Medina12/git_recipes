Lime and Cilantro Chicken

This recipe is for mouth watering Lime and Cilantro Chicken Thighs meant to be thrown on the grill. This recipe is good to feed about 2 people.

Ingridients

  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh squeezed Lime Juice
  • Fresh Chopped Cilantro
  • Red chili Flakes
  • 4 Cloves Garlic - minced
  • Brown Sugar
  • Groun Cumin
  • 6 Bone in Chicken thighs
  • salt and pepper to taste
