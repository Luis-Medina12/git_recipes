Orange Chicken

Description

What do you need to know? This is a classic chinese restaurant delicacy! Commonly unknown to have been an invented by the American chain restaurant Panda Express

Ingridients

  • Chicken Thigs for best flavor
  • 3 Eggs - whisked
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup Flour
  • 1 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • Rice vinegar
  • Ginger
  • Garlic Powder
  • Red Chili Flakes
  • Orange Zest
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. cook chicken thighs
  2. make orange sauce
  3. Thicken Sauce
  4. add chicken to sauce
  5. Serve with onions sprinkled on
