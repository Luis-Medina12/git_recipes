Carne Asada Tacos

Description

Tasty tacos filled with Carne Asada (Grilled Steak) and complimenting toppings.

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada: Skirt steak works for this
  • Corn Tortillas: fresh made from a local torilleria or pre-packaged
  • 1 bundle of Cilantro: diced
  • 1 Onion: thinly sliced
  • Avocado: Commonly known as "Oro Verde

Steps

  1. Grill steak for apprx 5 minutes on each side
  2. while the meat is grilling, satue onions on lard until golden brown
  3. heat up torillas on grill for a minute on each side
  4. bring it all together and enjoy
